## Serious Tools

SERIOUS TOOLS

Runs deeper than it looks

### Fine-Tune Mode

Double-tap any knob and it slows down. Same gesture, fraction of the movement. For the moments when a single step is too much.

### Bidirectional MIDI

Knobs follow your DAW, not just the other way around. Move a parameter in Ableton, the knob moves with it. Always in sync, never out of position.

### Per-Knob Configuration

Every knob has its own CC number, MIDI channel, label, and range. 64 knobs, 64 independent setups. Nothing is global unless you want it to be.

### Output Curves

Linear, Exponential, or Logarithmic scaling per follower. The same physical movement, completely different response. Dial in exactly how the control feels.

### Notes Mode

16 pads per bank with 29 scales and per-octave transpose. Drag for velocity, pitch bend, or full MPE expression. A playable instrument, not just a controller.

### Auto-Save

Everything saves automatically, every time. Close the app, switch presets, put the phone in your pocket — nothing is lost. Touch it again exactly where you left it.

## FAQ

FAQ

Questions before you buy?

### How low is the latency?

Typical end-to-end response is around 2ms over USB, about 8ms over Bluetooth, and roughly 10ms over Wi-Fi on modern iPhone and iPad hardware.

### Is this a subscription or a one-time purchase?

One-time purchase. No subscription, no tiers, and every feature is included.

### Will it work with my DAW?

It works with Ableton Live, Logic Pro, FL Studio, Bitwig, MainStage, and a wide range of MIDI apps and hardware setups.

### Does it work over Wi-Fi?

Yes. Knob16 works over Wi-Fi in addition to USB and Bluetooth MIDI.

### What's 14-bit MIDI?

Standard CC gives you 128 steps. 14-bit CC pairs MSB and LSB messages for 16,384 steps, which makes fine control noticeably smoother.

### What's MPE?

MPE is MIDI Polyphonic Expression. Each note gets its own expressive lane for pitch, pressure, and slide, which is how touchscreen performance becomes playable.

### iPhone or iPad?

The product story is built around iPhone, and iPad is supported as a larger secondary surface.

### Can I use it live without a laptop?

Yes, if your destination hardware or app accepts MIDI input. Knob16 can drive synths and mobile setups directly.
